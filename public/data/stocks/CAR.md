# Carreras Group Limited (CAR)

## Company Overview
Carreras Group Limited is Jamaica's dominant tobacco company and a subsidiary of British American Tobacco (BAT). Operating since 1962, Carreras manufactures and distributes cigarettes under the iconic Craven A, Matterhorn, and Benson & Hedges brands. Despite declining global tobacco trends, Carreras maintains exceptional profitability through pricing power and strong free cash flow generation.
