# Lasco Financial Services Limited (LASC)

## Company Overview
Lasco Financial Services Limited is a Jamaica Stock Exchange Junior Market listed company providing cambio (foreign exchange), remittance, and microfinancing services across Jamaica. Part of the LASCO Group of Companies, LASC operates over 40 service locations island-wide. The company benefits from high remittance volumes into Jamaica — one of the world's highest per-capita remittance recipient countries.
