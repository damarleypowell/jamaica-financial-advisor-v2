# Barita Investments Limited (BIL)

## Company Overview
Barita Investments Limited is one of Jamaica's fastest-growing full-service investment banks and asset management firms. Founded in 1977 and transformed under new ownership since 2018, Barita offers brokerage, structured products, private equity, foreign exchange, and wealth management services. The company has grown its AUM (Assets Under Management) from $40B to over $380B JMD in seven years.
