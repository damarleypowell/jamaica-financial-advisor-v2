# Stationery and Office Supplies Limited (SOS)

## Company Overview
Stationery and Office Supplies Limited is Jamaica's largest retail and wholesale stationery, office supplies, and educational materials company. Operating 6 retail locations and an extensive B2B corporate division, SOS serves schools, government agencies, corporations, and individual consumers. The company holds exclusive distribution rights for major brands including Pilot, Avery, and HP office supplies in Jamaica.
