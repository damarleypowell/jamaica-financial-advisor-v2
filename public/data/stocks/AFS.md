# Access Financial Services Limited (AFS)

## Company Overview
Access Financial Services Limited is Jamaica's largest listed microfinance institution, providing personal and business loans to underserved segments of the Jamaican market. The company's innovative group lending and weekly repayment model maintains one of the lowest non-performing loan ratios in the sector. AFS operates 28 branches island-wide and has disbursed over $100B JMD in loans since inception in 1998.
