# iCreate Limited (ICREATE)

## Company Overview
iCreate Limited is Jamaica's leading technology education, digital marketing, and creative media company. The company operates coding bootcamps, digital skills training, web development, and content creation services. iCreate's training programs have placed over 8,000 graduates in tech jobs regionally. The company also operates a digital agency providing social media management, video production, and brand strategy to corporate clients across Jamaica and the Caribbean.
