# Caribbean Producers Jamaica Limited (CPJ)

## Company Overview
Caribbean Producers Jamaica Limited is the island's premier food and beverage import and distribution company, supplying hotels, restaurants, airlines, and retail chains with premium international products. CPJ represents over 200 global food brands and is the exclusive Jamaican distributor for products including Heineken, Diageo premium spirits, and numerous specialty food lines. The company is a direct beneficiary of Jamaica's booming tourism sector.
