# Sagicor Financial Corporation (SFC)

## Company Overview
Sagicor Financial Corporation is one of the Caribbean's oldest and most established financial services groups, tracing its roots to 1840. Operating across 23 countries, Sagicor provides life insurance, health insurance, annuities, pension administration, and commercial banking. Its Jamaica operations are anchored by Sagicor Bank and Sagicor Life Jamaica.
