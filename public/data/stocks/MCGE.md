# Margaritaville Caribbean Group (MCGE)

## Company Overview
Margaritaville Caribbean Group operates the iconic Margaritaville chain of beach bars, restaurants, and tourist entertainment complexes across Jamaica and the Caribbean. The company holds the exclusive franchise rights to the Margaritaville brand in the Caribbean region. Key locations include Margaritaville Montego Bay, Margaritaville Ocho Rios, and Margaritaville Negril, each attracting hundreds of thousands of cruise ship and stopover tourists annually.
