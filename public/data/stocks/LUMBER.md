# Lumber Depot Limited (LUMBER)

## Company Overview
Lumber Depot Limited is Jamaica's largest retail building materials chain, operating 8 stores island-wide. The company supplies lumber, hardware, plumbing, electrical, and construction materials to contractors, developers, and DIY homeowners. As Jamaica's housing sector booms — with a government target of 70,000 new affordable housing units by 2028 — Lumber Depot is directly positioned to benefit from sustained construction demand.
