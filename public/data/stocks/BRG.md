# Berger Paints Jamaica Limited (BRG)

## Company Overview
Berger Paints Jamaica Limited is Jamaica's dominant paint manufacturer and retailer, part of the global RPM International group. Founded in 1952, Berger produces and distributes architectural, decorative, industrial, and automotive paints through its network of Berger Color Centres and authorized dealers. The company holds approximately 65% market share of Jamaica's paint market and benefits from the construction and renovation boom.
