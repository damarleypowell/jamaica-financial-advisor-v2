# CAC 2000 Limited (CAC)

## Company Overview
CAC 2000 Limited is Jamaica's leading provider of HVAC (Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning), building automation, mechanical and electrical engineering services. The company designs, installs, and maintains climate control systems for hotels, hospitals, commercial buildings, and data centers across Jamaica and the Caribbean. CAC benefits from Jamaica's booming hotel construction pipeline and the growing demand for energy-efficient building systems.
