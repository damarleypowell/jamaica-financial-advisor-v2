# Productive Business Solutions Limited (PBS)

## Company Overview
Productive Business Solutions Limited is the Caribbean's largest technology and managed services company. PBS provides IT infrastructure, cloud solutions, cybersecurity, enterprise software, office automation, and digital transformation consulting to corporate clients across 9 Caribbean territories. The company is an authorized reseller for HP, Cisco, Microsoft, and SAP, and manages over $15B JMD in annual technology procurement for regional enterprises.
