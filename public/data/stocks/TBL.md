# Tropical Battery Company Limited (TBL)

## Company Overview
Tropical Battery Company Limited is Jamaica's leading distributor of automotive and industrial batteries, electrical accessories, and related products. The company distributes leading global battery brands including Exide, Energizer, and Duracell across Jamaica and the Eastern Caribbean. With the rise of EV vehicles and solar energy storage in Jamaica, Tropical Battery is pivoting toward lithium battery solutions and energy storage systems.
