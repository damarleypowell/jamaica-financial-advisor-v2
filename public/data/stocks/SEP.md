# Seprod Limited (SEP)

## Company Overview
Seprod Limited is one of Jamaica's leading manufacturing conglomerates, producing edible oils, fats, soaps, detergents, and dairy products. The company's brands — including Serv-Well, Supligen, and Sunny Isle — are household names across Jamaica and the Caribbean. Seprod also operates Facey Commodity, a major regional food distribution business, and Facey Telecom.
