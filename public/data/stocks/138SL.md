# 138 Student Living Jamaica Limited (138SL)

## Company Overview
138 Student Living Jamaica Limited is a real estate investment and development company focused on purpose-built student accommodation (PBSA) adjacent to Jamaican tertiary institutions. The company develops and operates modern student housing near the University of the West Indies (UWI), University of Technology (UTech), and other campuses, addressing a chronic student housing shortage estimated at 18,000+ beds island-wide.
