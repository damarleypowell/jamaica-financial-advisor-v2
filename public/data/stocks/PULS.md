# Pulse Investments Limited (PULS)

## Company Overview
Pulse Investments Limited is Jamaica's premier entertainment, fashion, and media company. Founded by Kingsley Cooper in 1985, Pulse manages Jamaica's top modeling agency, operates lifestyle media platforms, produces the internationally recognized Pulse Fashion Week, and manages entertainment assets including the Pulse Centre in New Kingston. The company's IP includes iconic Jamaican fashion and entertainment intellectual property.
