# Wisynco Group Limited (WISYNCO)

## Company Overview
Wisynco Group Limited is Jamaica's leading beverages and consumer goods company, best known as the exclusive manufacturer and distributor of Coca-Cola products on the island. Beyond beverages, Wisynco distributes international FMCG (fast-moving consumer goods) brands including Gatorade, Minute Maid, Powerade, and Heinz. The company's logistics network reaches 100% of Jamaica's retail points.
