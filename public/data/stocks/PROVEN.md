# Proven Investments Limited (PROVEN)

## Company Overview
Proven Investments Limited is a fast-growing Caribbean financial holding company providing wealth management, banking, real estate, and structured finance services. Operating in Jamaica, Cayman Islands, Belize, and Panama, Proven manages over $180B JMD in client assets. The company's investment thesis centers on delivering superior risk-adjusted returns in Caribbean markets where traditional institutions underserve sophisticated investors.
