# Main Event Entertainment Group Limited (MEEG)

## Company Overview
Main Event Entertainment Group Limited is Jamaica's leading event management, staging, and audiovisual production company. MEEG provides comprehensive event solutions including stage production, sound systems, lighting, branding, and digital streaming. The company's client roster includes major corporate brands, government agencies, and international artists performing in Jamaica. MEEG is the backbone of Jamaica's billion-dollar events industry.
