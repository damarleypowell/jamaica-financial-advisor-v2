# Caribbean Cement Company Limited (CCC)

## Company Overview
Caribbean Cement Company Limited is Jamaica's sole manufacturer of Portland cement, operating since 1952. A subsidiary of Trinidad Cement Limited (TCL), which is in turn part of the CRH global building materials group, CCC holds a dominant market position in Jamaica's construction materials sector. The company benefits directly from government infrastructure spending and the booming tourism construction pipeline.
