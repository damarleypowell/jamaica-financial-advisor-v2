# Express Catering Limited (ECL)

## Company Overview
Express Catering Limited operates the exclusive food and beverage concession rights at Sangster International Airport in Montego Bay, Jamaica — the country's busiest airport with over 4 million passenger throughput annually. ECL manages iconic Jamaica Blue Coffee shops, Island Grill fast food outlets, and premium duty-free dining concepts. Revenue scales directly with international tourist arrivals.
