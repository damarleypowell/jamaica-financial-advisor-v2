# Mayberry Investments Limited (MJE)

## Company Overview
Mayberry Investments Limited is a Jamaica-based licensed securities dealer and investment bank offering brokerage, portfolio management, equity club investments, and corporate finance advisory services. Mayberry pioneered the Equity Club concept allowing retail investors to pool funds and access shares in pre-IPO and primary market offerings. The company is a major arranger of IPOs on the JSE.
