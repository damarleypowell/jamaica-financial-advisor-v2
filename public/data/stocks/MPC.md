# Mailpac Group Limited (MPC)

## Company Overview
Mailpac Group Limited is Jamaica's leading e-commerce logistics and international parcel delivery company. Mailpac enables Jamaicans to shop from US, UK, and Canadian online retailers by providing mailbox addresses, consolidation, freight forwarding, and island-wide last-mile delivery. As e-commerce adoption in Jamaica grows rapidly, Mailpac is the dominant infrastructure enabling international online shopping for Jamaican consumers.
