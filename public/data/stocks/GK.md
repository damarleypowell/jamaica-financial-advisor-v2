# GraceKennedy Limited (GK)

## Company Overview
GraceKennedy Limited is one of Jamaica's and the Caribbean's leading conglomerates, founded in 1922. Operating across food and beverage, banking, insurance, money services, and remittances, GraceKennedy's brands include Grace Foods, Western Union Jamaica, First Global Bank, and KeyInsurance. The company has distribution in over 60 countries and is synonymous with Jamaican food culture internationally.
