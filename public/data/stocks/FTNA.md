# Fontana Pharmacy Limited (FTNA)

## Company Overview
Fontana Pharmacy Limited is Jamaica's leading private pharmacy and health retail chain, operating 10 full-service pharmacy stores across Kingston, St. Andrew, and St. Catherine. Beyond traditional pharmaceutical dispensing, Fontana's stores feature health and wellness products, cosmetics, optical services, and nutritional supplements. The company is expanding its footprint into corporate healthcare benefits management and digital prescription services.
