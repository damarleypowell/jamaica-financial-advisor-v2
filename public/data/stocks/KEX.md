# Knutsford Express Services Limited (KEX)

## Company Overview
Knutsford Express Services Limited is Jamaica's premium intercity passenger transportation company, operating luxury coach services between Kingston, Montego Bay, Ocho Rios, Negril, and other major destinations. Known for punctuality, comfort, and executive-class service, Knutsford Express targets business travelers, tourists, and middle-class commuters. The company also offers courier and logistics services.
