# Lasco Distributors Limited (LASD)

## Company Overview
Lasco Distributors Limited is Jamaica's leading consumer goods distribution company and part of the LASCO Group. The company distributes a portfolio of household brands including LASCO food drink products, whey proteins, and health supplements, as well as third-party international brands. LASD's nationwide distribution network reaches over 18,000 retail points including supermarkets, pharmacies, wholesalers, and informal vendors.
