# Indies Pharma Jamaica Limited (INDIES)

## Company Overview
Indies Pharma Jamaica Limited is Jamaica's leading pharmaceutical wholesale distributor, supplying hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, and healthcare institutions with branded and generic medicines, medical devices, and healthcare consumables. The company is the exclusive distributor for over 40 international pharmaceutical brands in Jamaica and represents manufacturers from Europe, North America, India, and China. Indies Pharma is a critical link in Jamaica's healthcare supply chain.
