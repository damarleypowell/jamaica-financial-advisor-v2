# Scotia Group Jamaica Limited (SGJAM)

## Company Overview
Scotia Group Jamaica Limited is a financial holding company and subsidiary of The Bank of Nova Scotia (Scotiabank), one of Canada's largest banks. Operating in Jamaica since 1889, Scotia Group provides retail and commercial banking, insurance, and investment management services. The group holds a significant portion of the Jamaican mortgage and personal loan market.
