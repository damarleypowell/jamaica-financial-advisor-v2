# Jamaica Broilers Group Limited (JBGL)

## Company Overview
Jamaica Broilers Group Limited is the Caribbean's leading poultry and animal feed producer. Founded in 1958, the company operates Jamaica's largest integrated poultry business — from hatcheries and feed milling to processing and distribution. Its flagship brand "Best Dressed Chicken" dominates the Jamaican market. The group also operates in Haiti and the United States.
