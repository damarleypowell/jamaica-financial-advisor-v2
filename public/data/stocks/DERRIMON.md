# Derrimon Trading Company Limited (DERRIMON)

## Company Overview
Derrimon Trading Company Limited is one of Jamaica's leading wholesale, distribution, and retail food companies. The company distributes over 3,000 consumer product lines to supermarkets, wholesalers, and institutions across Jamaica. Derrimon also operates Sampars Cash & Carry supermarket chain and Select Grocers retail stores. Its recent acquisition of the Caribbean Flavor Foods brand has expanded its proprietary product portfolio significantly.
