# NCB Financial Group Limited (NCBFG)

## Company Overview
NCB Financial Group Limited is Jamaica's largest financial conglomerate, operating across banking, insurance, wealth management, and pension fund administration. Founded in 1977, NCB serves over 800,000 customers across Jamaica and the wider Caribbean through its flagship National Commercial Bank and subsidiaries including Clarien Group and Guardian Holdings.
