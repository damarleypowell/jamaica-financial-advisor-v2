# Sygnus Credit Investments Limited (SCI)

## Company Overview
Sygnus Credit Investments Limited is a credit-focused alternative investment company listed on the Jamaica Stock Exchange. SCI provides private credit solutions — including senior secured loans, mezzanine financing, and structured credit — to mid-market companies across the Caribbean. The company fills a critical gap in regional capital markets, funding businesses unable to access traditional bank credit at competitive rates.
