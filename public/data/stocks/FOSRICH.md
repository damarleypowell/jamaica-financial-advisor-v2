# Fosrich Company Limited (FOSRICH)

## Company Overview
Fosrich Company Limited is Jamaica's leading distributor of electrical, solar, lighting, and building materials products. The company imports and distributes over 8,000 product lines from major international brands, supplying electrical contractors, solar installers, hardware retailers, and construction companies. As Jamaica transitions toward renewable energy, Fosrich's solar product division has become its fastest-growing segment.
